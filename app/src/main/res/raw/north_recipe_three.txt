Canadian Mapple Taffy (Tire Sur La Neige)


Serves: 5-6
Time: 15 mins

Ingredients:

Maple syrup -- 1 quart
Fresh, clean snow -- a good amount


Directions:

1.  Bring the maple syrup to a boil in a large saucepan and simmer until it reaches 240Â°F on a candy thermometer.
2.  Pour in a thin stream over clean snow.
3.  Let set for a moment to begin to harden.
4.  Roll up on a clean stick and eat.
5.  Enjoy!
